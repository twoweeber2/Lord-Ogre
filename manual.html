Getting Started

This short guide will teach you the very basics on how to set up your OGRESWAMP!

Creating a swamp

Use the command /new to create a new swamp.

Training

Training ogres is vital for survival. Try to aim at around 20% of your population being farmers and 5% miners. Add Warriors as neccesary to prevent getting brawled. Use /Assign to start training ogres.
